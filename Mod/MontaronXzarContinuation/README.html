Montaron & Xzar Continuation

Before getting into each component I want to quickly share my philosophy behind this mod. My intention is for my added content to blend in with the original as smooth as possible. Meaning for example that there are no extensive friendship or romance talks. My added companions will behave very similar to original companions and add a similar amount of content.

If you find a bug or have feedback/questions, please reach out to me. I am grateful for feedback of any kind. (if you keep it civil :) )

Easiest and fastest way to reach me is my Discord. If that's not your thing, feel free to reach out per mail to janwburner@gmail.com.

Components

Montaron & Xzar for SoD

If Montaron and Xzar were adventuring with you during Baldur's Gate I, you will now have the chance to meet them in Siege of Dragonspear. They will join you and comment on a handful of events.

They are a bit barebones and I don't guarantee to change that. Compared to vanilla companions they miss banters and interjections. They also miss an interaction with Thrixx.

Montaron for SoA & ToB

Montaron will be available in Shadows of Amn and Throne of Bhaal. To get started take a look around the Docks after Xzar's Quest.

In the first dialogue with Montaron in SoA your first answer will decide whether Montaron was with you in BG1 (if you use EET, there might only be one answer)

Montaron will every now and then comment on dialogues or a quest and there are a handful of "random" talks, but there are no constant friendship talks. He should have a similar amount of content as for example Korgan. There is also at least one banter between every companion and Montaron.

This component also adds two new small encounters to the game, that only occur if you decide to take Montaron into your party. If you do not take Montaron, the game is completely unchanged.

Why only Montaron? As every pair is split up in the original, I thought it would be reasonable to split up Montaron and Xzar. There are mods that bring back both, if you are interested in that (they are of course incompatible with mine)

Portraits for non-companion NPCs

Adds AI-generated portraits to non-companion characters added by this mod, in case you use a 'portraits for everyone' mod, so they don't stand out.

Compatibility

I'll try to add more crossmod content in the future.

EET

The mod should be fully compatible with EET and Montaron will behave as Minsc or Jaheira do with EET.

For example stats added by tomes or proficiencies carry over to BG II
