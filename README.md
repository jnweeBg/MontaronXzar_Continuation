# Montaron & Xzar Continuation

If you found a bug or have feedback/questions, feel free to reach out to me.

Easiest and fastest way to reach me is my [Discord](https://discord.gg/yGSAtZuzBq). If that's not your thing, feel free to reach out per mail to janwburner@gmail.com.

## Components

The main addition by this mod is adding Montaron as a companion to Baldur's Gate II, but you can also add Montaron & Xzar to SoD and some smaller components.

More detailed descriptions of each component are in the README.html, that you can choose to open before installing with WEIDU or open after downloading the mod (Mod/\<ModName\>/README.html).

## Compatibility

I'll try to add more crossmod content in the future.

### EET
The mod should be fully compatible with EET and Montaron will behave as Minsc or Jaheira do with EET.

*For example stats added by tomes or proficiencies carry over to Baldur's Gate II*
